Welcome to SMK Kristen Immanuel

SMK Kristen Immanuel is a vocational high school dedicated to providing quality education in three major fields: Accounting, Computer Science, and Marketing. The school aims to equip students with practical skills and knowledge to excel in their chosen careers.

With a strong emphasis on both theoretical learning and hands-on experience, SMK Kristen Immanuel prepares students to meet industry demands. The Accounting program focuses on financial management and bookkeeping, the Computer Science program delves into programming and IT solutions, and the Marketing program trains students in business strategies and consumer engagement.

SMK Kristen Immanuel continues to foster a learning environment that encourages innovation, professionalism, and excellence in every field.
